How to Play

  1. Choose one of your non-empty pits.
  2. Sow counter-clockwise, skipping your opponent's store.
  3. Land in your store for another turn.
  4. Land in an empty pit on your side to capture opposite stones.
  5. Empty one side to end the game; highest store wins.
